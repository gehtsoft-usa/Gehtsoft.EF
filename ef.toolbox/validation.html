Validation Toolkit

The validation toolkit consists of three packages:

  • Gehtsoft.Validation for validating arbitrary data.

  • Gehtsoft.EF.Validation for specially validating Gehtsoft.EF entities.

  • Gehtsoft.EF.Mapper.Validation for specially validating mapper models.

The whole idea of the validation toolkit is similar to FluentValidation, however the package was developed to overcome the FluentValidation limitations to create validation map on the fly, using entities and models meta data.
