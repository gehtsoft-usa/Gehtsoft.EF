class ConditionBuilder

Parents

object

IOpBracketAcceptor

Brief

The build of the condition.

Details

The most convenient way to use the condition builder is to add SingleConditionBuilder for each individual condition using ConditionBuilder.Add or ConditionBuilder.AddGroup methods.

The rest of methods are designed for EF infrastructure or may be used to construct complex queries that aren't supported by EF.

Use also ConditionBuilderExtension to easier and more readable definition of the conditions.

The condition builder is used to build where, on and having clauses of SQL queries.
